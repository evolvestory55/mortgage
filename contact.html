Before You Write — Quick Answers

01 Can you tell me what mortgage rate I'll qualify for?
No — we are not a lender, broker, or financial institution. We cannot provide rate quotes, pre-approvals, or loan offers. We are a free, independent calculator platform. To get a real rate quote, contact a licensed mortgage lender or use our free Mortgage Calculator and Loan Comparison tool to understand what different rates mean for your monthly payment before you talk to a lender.
02 The calculator result doesn't match my lender's quote — why?
This is normal. Our calculators provide estimates based on the inputs you enter. Lender quotes include additional factors like origination fees, discount points, escrow account requirements, specific PMI rates tied to your credit score, and exact property tax figures from the county assessor. Our results are accurate for the inputs given — but your lender's Loan Estimate is the official document. Use our tools for planning; use your lender's LE for final decisions.
03 I found a bug — what information should I include?
Thank you — accuracy is our top priority. Please include: (1) the calculator name (e.g. Refinance Calculator, DTI Calculator), (2) the exact inputs you entered, (3) the result you got, (4) what result you expected, and (5) your browser and device (e.g. Chrome on iPhone 15). This helps us reproduce and fix the issue quickly. We address all confirmed formula bugs within 24–48 hours.
04 Can I suggest a new calculator or feature?
Absolutely — we love feature requests. Tools currently on the roadmap include a HELOC calculator, ARM (adjustable-rate mortgage) payment tracker, home equity loan calculator, and closing cost estimator by state. If you need something specific, describe your use case in the message form and we'll prioritize it. Our most requested tools have always come directly from user feedback.
