Privacy Policy

How we handle the minimal data we collect — and why your financial information never reaches us at all.

01Who We Are

Mortgage & Loans ("we," "our," or "us") is a free, online-only mortgage and loan calculator platform. We are not a bank, mortgage lender, loan originator, broker, or financial institution of any kind. We do not issue loans, process loan applications, originate credit, or connect users with lenders.

This Privacy Policy applies to all pages and tools hosted on this website. By using the site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02What This Site Does

This website provides free, browser-based financial calculators for educational and planning purposes only. These tools include: mortgage payment calculators, amortization schedule generators, refinance calculators, affordability estimators, debt-to-income ratio calculators, rent vs buy comparisons, biweekly and extra payment calculators, loan comparison tools, and construction loan calculators.

All calculations are performed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No calculation input — including loan amounts, income figures, interest rates, property values, or any other financial data — is ever transmitted to our servers, stored in a database, or accessible to us in any way. This is a technical fact, not merely a policy promise.

03Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not collect, because we know users of financial tools are rightly concerned about privacy:

  • We do not collect your name, address, or date of birth.
  • We do not collect your Social Security Number (SSN) or Tax ID.
  • We do not collect income, employment, or asset information.
  • We do not collect credit scores or credit history.
  • We do not collect bank account or payment information.
  • We do not collect property addresses or purchase details.
  • We do not perform credit checks of any kind — soft or hard.
  • We do not share data with lenders or financial partners.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.

The numbers you enter into any calculator on this site exist only in your browser's memory while you are on the page. When you leave or close the tab, they are gone.

04Data We Do Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate and improve the site:

Usage & Analytics Data: We use Microsoft Clarity (a behavioral analytics tool by Microsoft) to understand how users interact with the site. This may include pages visited, time on page, clicks, scroll depth, and anonymized device/browser information. This data does not include any financial inputs you enter into calculators. See Section 07 for details.

Contact Form Data: If you choose to contact us using the contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the message you submit. This information is used only to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list or share this data with third parties.

Advertising Data: We display ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. See Section 06 for details.

05How We Use Collected Data

We use the limited data we collect for the following purposes only:

  • To respond to messages submitted through our contact form.
  • To analyze and improve site usability based on anonymized behavioral analytics.
  • To display relevant third-party advertisements through Google AdSense, which funds the free operation of this platform.

We do not use your data for marketing emails, profiling, credit decisions, or any automated decision-making that affects you.

06Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

This website is monetized through Google AdSense. Google AdSense uses cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to serve advertisements that may be personalized based on your prior browsing behavior across the web.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at https://adssettings.google.com. You can also opt out through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page at https://optout.networkadvertising.org.

We do not control the content of ads served by Google. We do not receive any personal information from Google about individual users as a result of advertising. For more information on how Google uses data in advertising, visit https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

By using this site, you consent to the use of advertising cookies as described above. If you do not wish to receive personalized ads, please use the opt-out links above.

07Analytics (Microsoft Clarity)

We use Microsoft Clarity, a free behavioral analytics service provided by Microsoft Corporation. Clarity collects anonymized data about how users navigate and interact with our website — including mouse movements, clicks, scroll behavior, and pages visited. This helps us identify usability issues and improve the calculator experience.

Clarity does not record any financial data you enter into calculators. It does not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, through the contact form).

Microsoft processes Clarity data in accordance with Microsoft's Privacy Policy (https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement). You can opt out of Clarity's data collection by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser or by using a browser extension that blocks Microsoft's tracking scripts.

08Cookies

This website uses cookies in the following categories:

Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are required for the site to function. They do not store personal information and cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies: Placed by Microsoft Clarity to collect anonymized usage data as described in Section 07. These can be blocked via browser settings or privacy extensions.

Advertising Cookies: Placed by Google AdSense to deliver and measure advertising as described in Section 06. You may opt out of personalized advertising using the links in Section 06.

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the display of advertisements but will not affect the functionality of any calculator on this site.

09Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR for EU residents and CCPA for California residents):

  • Right to know what data we hold about you.
  • Right to request deletion of your data.
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal data (note: we do not sell personal data).
  • Right to opt out of personalized advertising (see Section 06).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@mortgageandloans.com. Because we collect very limited data, most requests can be resolved quickly. We do not have a way to identify you from calculator usage data, as we do not collect any.

10Children's Privacy

This website is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our contact form, please contact us immediately at support@mortgageandloans.com and we will delete the information promptly.

When we update it, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
