01Introduction

Welcome to Mortgage & Loans Finance ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your data when you use our mortgage and loan services platform.

By accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

02Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Identification Information

  • Full legal name, date of birth, Social Security Number (SSN) or Tax ID
  • Government-issued ID details
  • Contact information: email address, phone number, mailing address

2.2 Financial Information

  • Income, employment status, and employer details
  • Credit history and credit score data
  • Bank account and asset information
  • Existing debt obligations and liabilities
  • Loan application details (loan type, amount, purpose)

2.3 Property Information (for Mortgage Applications)

  • Property address, type, and estimated value
  • Purchase price or refinance details
  • Homeowner's insurance and tax information

2.4 Technical & Usage Data

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Pages visited, time spent on site, and clickstream data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Referral source (how you found us)

2.5 Communication Data

  • Messages sent through our contact forms or support chat
  • Email correspondence with our team
  • Recorded calls (with your consent, where required by law)

04Credit Checks & Third-Party Verification

By submitting a loan or mortgage application, you authorize us to:

  • Perform soft and/or hard credit inquiries through credit bureaus (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion)
  • Verify your employment and income through third-party verification services
  • Share your information with partner lenders and financial institutions for underwriting purposes
  • Access your banking data through secure open banking connections (if applicable)
Important: Hard credit inquiries may temporarily impact your credit score. We will notify you before any hard pull is performed.

05Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with:

  • Lending Partners & Underwriters — to evaluate and fund your loan
  • Credit Bureaus — for credit verification and reporting
  • Service Providers — hosting, analytics, cybersecurity vendors bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Authorities — when required by law, court order, or regulatory body (e.g., CFPB, FDIC)
  • Business Successors — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

06Data Retention

We retain your personal and financial information for:

  • Active accounts: Duration of the account relationship
  • Closed applications: Up to 7 years to comply with federal lending laws (ECOA, HMDA, RESPA)
  • Marketing data: Until you opt out or withdraw consent
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for specific retention periods

07Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your data:

256-bit SSL/TLS encryption
Bank-level security
Regular penetration testing
Strict access controls
Employee confidentiality agreements
Note: Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and report any suspicious activity immediately.

08Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-Out — Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights
California Residents (CCPA): You have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Please see our CCPA Addendum for details.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@[yourdomain].com

09Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in and remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and performance
  • Deliver relevant content and loan products

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

10Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has submitted data, we will delete it promptly.
